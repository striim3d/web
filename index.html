Welcome to Striim3D!

We are a group of passionate developers aiming to restore functionality of 3DS media applications.

Started out as a joke between some of the old WiiLink team and left dormant for half a decade, Striim3D (formerly 3DSLink) returns! Access the panel above to navigate to other areas of the site, such as the current list of services we support.

Our main focus currently is Nintendo Video, SpotPass TV, and other applications taking advantage of the "EASTER" framework. We are also creating tools to assist with creation of video for these platforms!
